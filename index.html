Project Mageral

⠀

A project management app that can keep track of you and your team's deadlines and tasks. Allows easy and clear communication between members, easy access to important documents, and allows users to track their team member's progress. It uses the Python Tkinter module for UI and Firebase's firestore and authentication for its backend.

⠀

Developed over 3 months by my partner, Tan Zi Yi, and myself.

Balloon Sattelite Project

⠀

The goal of this project is to create a functioning, mini-satellite. When sent into the air via a balloon, it will accurately collect data, such as temperature altitude. This is then transmitted to a station on the ground and stored in an SD card attatched to the device. Additionally, it has to be lightweight and sturdy to withstand high wind speeds. The device was designed with several microcontrollers and arduinos, custom programmed to do the aforementioned tasks.

⠀

In the end, the project was a huge success, our satellite flew up, accurately collected data, and arrived back down, intact. This is detailed in our report here.

Electronic Lock System

⠀

This circuit detects when there is motion from the LDR, and after 5 seconds, activates a full alarm, while ensuring that the theif is unable to disable it. Additionally, there is a combination lock that allows the owner to be able to disarm the system within the 5 seconds.

⠀

Works as intended, both simulated and in real life.
